Detachment History

The Air Force Reserve Officers Training Corps Detachment 195 was originally installed as Detachment #36 at Illinois Institute of Technology on May 9, 1951. This was the result of General Order 104, which called for more training detachments.

Today Detachment 195 hosts more then 10 cross-town schools in the Chicago-Land Area. The majority of cadets in Detachment 195 come from these cross-town schools. Some cadets travel over an hour in Chicago traffic to come to IIT for AFROTC activities. For this reason, Warhawks are the most motivated cadets who go above and beyond in everything they do. Detachment 195 continues to improve and shape college students into the best Air Force Officers our military needs today.
